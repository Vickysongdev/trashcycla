4. What is the difference between an Agent and a collector?

Ans: As a Trashcycla agent you provide us space for storage of sorted waste collected. And work with collectors and producers to recover recyclable material while a collector mobile waste retriver (Like uber drivers) who goes about(with a Cart) using our technology(app) and (IOT scale) to pick up waste and pay the producers per KG for items collected.

5. How do I become an Agent?

Ans: i. Sign up directly on our website or download our agent app on google playstore, and fill out out your details. ii. Complete your documentation and Know Your Customer (KYC) process. iii. Go through our Physical or in-app segmented training module videos iv. Get the IoT Scale Asset shipped to you or pick it up at any of our closest office/ outlet Immediately you get activated, an account manager is attached to you, to help grow and scale your new recycling business with the support of the Trashcycla africa technology. Join the ride!

6. How much is required to start?

Ans: This is your Mini-recycling business. To start you must set up capital that will be used in purchasing items from either a collector or producer. Usually this is around 30,000Naira to 50,000Naira weekly.

7. What is the minimum quantity of recyclables that i have to have stored?

Ans: Agent are required to have a minimum cumulative kilogram volume of 500KG before requesting for offtake. But because we do route pickup schedules, even when you have less than the minimum offtake Kilogram, your collected recyclables can be picked up when offtake route is along your outlet lane, you would have also recieved a prior in-app and SMS notification.
